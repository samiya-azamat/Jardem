The Problem

Stroke leaves millions without access to proper rehabilitation

👨‍⚕️

No Therapist Nearby

Most stroke survivors in rural areas have no access to a qualified rehabilitation specialist. Without guidance, patients don't know if they are exercising correctly.Recovery stalls silently.

🚗

Costly Clinic Visits

Transport costs, long distances, and session fees make regular clinic attendance impossible for most patients.Each missed visit is lost recovery time.

📋

No Clinical Record

Doctors have no way of knowing what patients actually do at home between appointments.Recovery is guessed, not measured.

The Gap

Existing solutions don't solve this

  • Current devices — SyreboCare ($300–600), SaeboGlove ($200–300) — are expensive, depend on imported parts, cannot be repaired locally, and generate no clinical data for doctors.

  • Existing apps — Kaia Health, MedBridge — require stable internet, digital literacy, and a therapist online. Inaccessible for rural patients.

"There is no tool that bridges home rehabilitation and clinical oversight — one that makes home sessions as measurable and accountable as clinic visits, without requiring the patient to travel or the doctor to be online."

Our Solution

Rehabilitation that watches, corrects, and records

01
📷

Real-Time Movement Correction

The camera watches your hand during every exercise and tells you instantly if you're doing it right. The same feedback a physiotherapist gives — available at home, any time.

02
📊

Automatic Clinical Reports

After every session the app records range of motion, rep count, and movement quality. Every four weeks it compiles a one-page clinical report — ready for the patient's next doctor appointment.

03
🚨

Instant Safety Escalation

If the patient reports pain or the camera detects dangerous movement — the app immediately stops and displays the doctor's phone number. No waiting. No delay.

No wearables. No special equipment. Just a smartphone.

How It Works

Four steps. No clinic needed.

01

Doctor sets the plan

Before the patient leaves hospital, the doctor assigns a rehabilitation programme in Jardem.Takes five minutes. Happens once.

02

Patient exercises at home

Every day the patient follows guided exercises while the camera watches in real time.Correcting movement, counting reps.

03

App records silently

Range of motion, accuracy, and session completion are logged automatically after every session.The patient does nothing extra.

04

Doctor sees real data

The patient arrives with a one-page Jardem report already generated.No guessing. No relying on memory.

From hospital discharge to follow-up — every session tracked, every movement measured.

The Technology

What makes this possible

🖐

Real-Time Hand Tracking

MediaPipe detects 21 hand landmarks from the phone camera — tracking finger angles, range of motion, and speed. No wearables required.

📝

Automatic Clinical Logging

Every session is recorded into a structured clinical note. ROM, rep count, movement quality, and pain flags compile into a report formatted for doctor review.

🔒

On-Device Processing

All video analysis happens locally on the phone. No video is ever sent to a server. Privacy protected by design — works fully offline.

Safety First

Built for vulnerable patients — with no compromises

✋

Auto-Stop

If pain is reported twice, the exercise is immediately paused. Won't resume until the doctor reactivates it.

📞

Instant Escalation

When something goes wrong, the app stops immediately and displays the doctor's phone number for instant calling.

👨‍⚕️

Doctor in Control

The doctor sets the plan. The AI delivers within safe boundaries only.No clinical decision without doctor approval.

🔒

Privacy Protected

No video is stored or transmitted. All processing is local. Patient data never leaves the device. Privacy by design, not by promise.

ℹ️

Clear Messaging

Every screen includes a clear disclaimer — Jardem is a support tool, not a replacement for clinical care. Transparency builds trust with patients and doctors.
